# 메일/메신저 답변 초안 작성

## Goal

업무 요청, 문의, 협조 메일에 대해 상황별 톤을 맞춘 답변 초안을 작성합니다.

## Workflow

1. Read the user's request, business context, recipient relationship, and attached material summary.
2. Identify the reply goal, required facts, tone, and any sensitive wording before drafting.
3. Produce a concise reply draft that can be pasted into email, messenger, or collaboration tools.
4. Mark assumptions clearly and list follow-up checks when the source material is incomplete.

## Output Shape

- 추천 답변 초안
- 톤/표현 조정 포인트
- 누락 정보 확인 사항
- 짧은 대체 문구

## Guardrails

- Do not invent commitments, approvals, dates, or numeric facts.
- Flag 개인정보, 계약, 재무 수치, 인사 정보, and confidential material before reuse.
- Keep the tone practical, specific, and suitable for internal office work.
