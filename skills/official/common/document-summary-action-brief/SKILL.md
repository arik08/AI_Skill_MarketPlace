# 문서 요약 및 액션 정리

## Goal

긴 업무 문서를 핵심 요약, 의사결정 사항, 후속 액션으로 빠르게 정리합니다.

## Workflow

1. Read the user's request, business context, and attached material summary.
2. Separate facts, decisions, risks, and action items before drafting.
3. Produce a concise working output that can be pasted into a memo, email, meeting note, or report.
4. Mark assumptions clearly and list follow-up checks when the source material is incomplete.

## Output Shape

- 핵심 요약
- 결정/합의 사항
- 담당자별 액션 아이템
- 확인 필요 사항

## Guardrails

- Do not invent missing decisions, names, dates, or numeric facts.
- Flag 개인정보, 계약, 재무 수치, 인사 정보, and confidential material before reuse.
- Keep the tone practical, specific, and suitable for internal office work.
